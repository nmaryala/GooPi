The Office of Pre-Award Services (OPAS) and the Office of Post-Award Management (OPAM) oversee pre-award and post-award administration for sponsored research campus-wide. Research Compliance oversees all compliance areas under one coordinated unit including animal subjects research, human subjects research, research ethics, and research safety and security. Research and Engagement units offer a wide range of workshops and trainings to support campus researchers and staff. Over the spring and summer, we have been communicating regarding the U.S. government’s recent focus on faculty compliance with disclosure requirements, including disclosure of foreign engagements related to federally-funded research.
