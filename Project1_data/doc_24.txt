Campus tours leave from the information desk on the second floor of the Murray D. Lincoln Campus Center. The second floor of the campus center is directly accessible from the second level of the campus parking garage. The GPS address for the campus center is 1 Campus Center Way, Amherst, ma 01003. Campus tours are offered Monday through Friday at 11:00 a.m., 1:30 p.m., and 2:30 p.m., and on Saturdays at 11:00 a.m. and 1:30 p.m. Feel free to call the Visitors Center if you have additional questions, 413-545-0306. Guests can park in our campus parking garage. The GPS address for the campus center parking garage is 1 Campus Center Way, Amherst, MA 01003 and the cost is $1.50 per hour. To allow sufficient time to park and check-in, arrive at least 15 minutes prior to your first scheduled event. Your check in location will differ depending on the time and day of your event(s). The location and address was sent to you via your confirmation email. If you have any further questions feel free to call us at 413-545-0306.
